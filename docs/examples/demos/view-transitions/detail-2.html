Ocean Depths

The ocean covers more than 70% of Earth's surface, yet we've explored less than 5% of its depths. Beneath the waves lies a world of mystery, from vibrant coral reefs to the crushing darkness of the abyssal plains.

Marine life has evolved incredible adaptations to survive in this alien environment. Bioluminescent creatures light up the darkness, while pressure-resistant organisms thrive in conditions that would crush surface life.

The ocean regulates our climate, produces half our oxygen, and holds secrets we're only beginning to understand.
