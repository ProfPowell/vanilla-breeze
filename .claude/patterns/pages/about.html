Our Story

We started with a simple observation: teams spend too much time on busywork and not enough time on the work that matters. So we set out to build something better.

What began as a side project in a small apartment has grown into a platform used by thousands of teams around the world. But our mission remains the same: to help people do their best work.

"We believe that when teams have the right tools, they can accomplish extraordinary things."

Today, we're a remote-first company with team members across 20 countries, united by our commitment to building products that make work more human.
