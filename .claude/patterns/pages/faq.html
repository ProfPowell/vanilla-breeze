Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our platform.

Getting Started

How do I create an account?

Creating an account is easy. Click the "Get Started" button and follow the signup process. You'll need to provide your email address and create a password. You can also sign up using your Google or GitHub account.

Is there a free trial?

Yes! All plans include a 14-day free trial with full access to all features. No credit card required to start your trial.

How do I invite my team members?

Go to Settings > Team and click "Invite Members." Enter their email addresses and select their role. They'll receive an invitation email with instructions to join.

Billing & Plans

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover) and PayPal. Enterprise customers can also pay by invoice or bank transfer.

Can I change my plan?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time from your account settings. Upgrades take effect immediately, and downgrades take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.

Do you offer refunds?

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee for all new subscriptions. If you're not satisfied, contact our support team within 30 days for a full refund.

Are there discounts for annual billing?

Yes! You save 20% when you choose annual billing instead of monthly. The discount is applied automatically at checkout.

Features & Usage

What integrations do you support?

We integrate with 100+ popular tools including Slack, Jira, GitHub, Figma, Google Workspace, Microsoft 365, and many more. Visit our integrations page for the full list.

Is there a mobile app?

Yes! Our mobile app is available for both iOS and Android. Download it from the App Store or Google Play Store to access your workspace on the go.

Can I export my data?

Absolutely. You can export all your data at any time in common formats (CSV, JSON, PDF). Go to Settings > Data Export to download your information.

Security & Privacy

Is my data secure?

Security is our top priority. We use AES-256 encryption for data at rest and TLS 1.3 for data in transit. We're SOC 2 Type II certified and undergo regular security audits.

Do you offer SSO?

Yes, SSO (Single Sign-On) is available on our Enterprise plan. We support SAML 2.0 and integrate with providers like Okta, Azure AD, and OneLogin.

Where is my data stored?

Your data is stored in secure data centers in the United States (AWS). Enterprise customers can choose data residency in the EU or other regions upon request.

Support

How do I contact support?

You can reach our support team via email at support@company.com, through in-app chat, or by submitting a ticket. Pro and Enterprise customers also have access to phone support.

What are your support hours?

Our support team is available Monday through Friday, 9am to 6pm Pacific Time. Enterprise customers have access to 24/7 support.

Still have questions?

Our team is here to help.
