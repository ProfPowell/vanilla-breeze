Frequently asked questions

What are your support hours?

Our support team is available Monday through Friday, 9am to 6pm Pacific Time. For urgent issues, we offer 24/7 emergency support for Enterprise customers.

How quickly will I get a response?

We typically respond to all inquiries within 24 business hours. Priority support customers receive responses within 4 hours.

Do you offer phone support?

Phone support is available for Pro and Enterprise customers. Starter plan customers have access to email and chat support.

Can I schedule a demo?

Absolutely! You can schedule a personalized demo with our sales team. Visit our demo page to book a time that works for you.
