Pricing FAQ

Can I change plans later?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express) as well as PayPal. Enterprise customers can also pay by invoice.

Is there a free trial?

Yes! All plans include a 14-day free trial. No credit card required to start.

Do you offer discounts for non-profits?

Yes, we offer a 50% discount for registered non-profit organizations. Contact our sales team for details.

What happens when my trial ends?

At the end of your trial, you'll be prompted to choose a plan. Your data will be preserved, and you can continue right where you left off.
