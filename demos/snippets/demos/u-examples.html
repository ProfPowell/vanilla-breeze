Spelling errors

Common misspellings: recieve (receive), seperate (separate), occured (occurred).

Spell-check demonstration

"I beleive this is definately the best approch."

Corrected: "I believe this is definitely the best approach."

Chinese proper names

Li Bai shi Tang dai zhu ming shi ren. (Li Bai was a famous Tang dynasty poet.)

Accessible spelling annotation

The word "recieve" (misspelled) should be spelled "receive."

VB underline styles compared

Wavy red — <u> (spelling error / annotation)

Solid green bottom border — <ins> (inserted text)

Solid blue underline — <a> (hyperlink)

Dotted muted — <abbr> (abbreviation)

Line-through muted — <s> (no longer relevant)

Line-through red + background — <del> (document edit)
