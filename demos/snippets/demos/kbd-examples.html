Single keys

Press Enter to submit. Press Esc to close. Press Tab to move focus.

Key combinations

Save: Ctrl + S

Command palette: Cmd + Shift + P

Undo: Ctrl + Z

Arrow keys

Navigate: ↑ ↓ ← →

Nested (key sequences)

In Vim: :wq to save and quit.
