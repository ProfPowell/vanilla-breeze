Animated Borders & Cursor Theming

Extension tokens for CSS-only animated borders (spin + pulse) and themeable cursors. All defaults are inert — themes opt in.

Spin Border

Rotating conic-gradient via @property --vb-border-angle. GPU-composited, zero JS.

Theme-aware

Uses --color-primary, --color-secondary, --color-accent. Swap the theme and the border follows.

Slow (6s)

Almost imperceptible motion. A quiet sign of life on dashboard widgets or ambient cards.

Rainbow (2.5s)

Full spectrum, 7 hue stops. Retro and kawaii themes use this; professional themes skip it.

Pulse Border

Animated box-shadow glow ring. Communicates active, selected, or alert states.

Primary pulse

Ring expands to --border-anim-pulse-size then fades. Selected or focused states.

Danger pulse

Red, faster (1.5s). Urgency without a modal. Critical inputs or system alerts.

Accent pulse

Larger spread (12px) with accent color. Override --border-anim-pulse-color and --border-anim-pulse-size.

Cursor Theming

SVG data URI cursors via --cursor-custom-* tokens. Hover each zone to see the cursor change.

Crosshair SVG ring + lines
Dot Orange dot, white center
Branded arrow Custom arrow in primary
Large a11y 48px ring, high visibility
Themes override --cursor-custom-default and --cursor-custom-pointer in their token block. The reset applies them to :root and interactive elements. SVG cursors use hex colors (SVG fill doesn't accept OKLCH in cursor URLs).

Reduced Motion

All animated borders respect prefers-reduced-motion: reduce and data-motion-reduced. When active, --border-anim-spin-duration and --border-anim-pulse-duration are set to 0s — the animation completes instantly, leaving a static border. No JS gating needed.
