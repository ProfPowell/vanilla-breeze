Pinned card stack

Scroll down — each card sticks to the top, stacking on top of the previous one.

First card

Pins at the top first. Stays pinned until the next card comes up and covers it.

Second card

Pins below the first by one offset increment, so a sliver of the previous card peeks out at the top.

Third card

Adds another offset. The pattern scales — keep adding cards and the offsets keep stacking.

Fourth card

The bottom of the stack. When this releases, the page continues normally.

The whole effect is pure CSS — position: sticky plus a per-card --i custom property driving the top offset. No JS, no scroll listeners.
