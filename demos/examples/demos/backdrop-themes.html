Capabilities

Rich Backgrounds

Multi-Region Elevation

Radius and shadow inherit from theme tokens — Swiss: sharp, Brutalist: offset, Organic: round.

Layout Integration

Works with data-page-layout grids — sidebar, holy-grail, and stack layouts.
