Catppuccin Mocha

Warm pastel colors on a tinted dark background. A cozy, approachable aesthetic with rounded shapes and unhurried motion.

Typography & Content

Catppuccin Mocha uses a rounded sans-serif feel for warmth and approachability. The medium border radius and slightly slower animations reinforce the cozy character.

The Catppuccin palette was created as a community-driven theme that values pastel colors and warmth. Mocha is the darkest of four flavors: Latte, Frappe, Macchiato, and Mocha.

Links use Catppuccin Blue and transition to Mauve on hover, creating a gentle color shift that feels natural and unforced.

Accordion: The Mocha Palette

Mocha features a rich set of named colors: Rosewater, Flamingo, Pink, Mauve, Red, Maroon, Peach, Yellow, Green, Teal, Sky, Sapphire, Blue, and Lavender. Each color has been tuned for harmony against the warm dark background.

Accordion: Community-Driven Design

Catppuccin is an open-source project maintained by a vibrant community. The theme is available for over 200 applications, making it one of the most widely adopted color schemes in the developer ecosystem.
