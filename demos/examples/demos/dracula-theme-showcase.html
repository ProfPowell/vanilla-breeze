Dracula Theme

A dark theme with vibrant purple, cyan, green, and pink accents. Designed for developers who prefer dark-first interfaces with expressive color.

Typography & Content

Dracula balances a dark background with carefully chosen high-contrast accents. The purple primary and pink accent create a distinctive identity that's immediately recognizable.

Originally created for Zeno Rocha's editor theme, Dracula has grown into a cross-application design system used across hundreds of tools and interfaces.

Links transition from purple to pink on hover, echoing the theme's signature color pairing. Motion is snappy at 0.8x duration for a responsive feel.

Accordion: The Dracula Palette

Dracula uses a carefully curated set of colors: a dark background (#282A36), current-line highlight (#44475A), foreground text (#F8F8F2), and comment gray (#6272A4). The accent colors — cyan, green, orange, pink, purple, red, and yellow — provide expressive syntax highlighting and UI accents.

Accordion: Dark-First Design

Unlike themes that treat dark mode as an afterthought, Dracula was designed dark-first. The light variant adapts the palette for bright environments while maintaining the same character and accent relationships.
