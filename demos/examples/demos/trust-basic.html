Provenance, Review & Status Indicators Demo

Human-Written

This content was written entirely by a human author. No visual indicator is shown by default, but the labeled class adds a badge.

AI-Assisted

This content was written by a human with AI tooling assistance. A subtle border accent indicates the collaboration.

AI-Generated

This content was primarily generated by AI. A background tint and border accent communicate the authorship model.

AI-Generated + Translated

AI-drafted in English then translated to another language. Extension tokens compose with a core value via space-separation.

Editor-Reviewed

This content has been reviewed and approved by an editor. Review is orthogonal to provenance — a page can be reviewed regardless of how it was authored.

Fact-Checked

Factual claims have been verified. Useful for evidence-backed content regardless of authorship model.

Draft

This content is work in progress. A dashed border and muted opacity indicate its provisional status. Status is orthogonal to provenance and review.

AI-Generated + Editor-Reviewed

AI-drafted and then reviewed by a human editor. Multiple attributes can decorate the same element since they answer different questions (how made / what review / publication status).
