SSR-Adopted Stats

The reading stats above were pre-rendered at build time by the SSG. When the <page-stats> component upgrades, it detects the existing <dl> markup and adopts it without re-rendering. This is the "wrap, don't replace" principle.

Disable JavaScript in your browser to verify the stats remain visible. The <time> element with datetime attribute is machine-readable even without script.
