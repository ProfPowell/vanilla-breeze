Every AI startup's landing page — purple gradients, ethereal glow, sparkle animations, and that unmistakable sense that a neural network designed the color palette.

Typography & Content

GenAI uses clean Inter-style typography with slightly wider heading letter-spacing for that futuristic feel. The deep void background lets luminous purple-blue glow effects shine.

The gradient mesh background layers radial blobs in purple, blue, and magenta. Headings emit a soft purple glow through layered text-shadow, while HR elements shimmer with a moving gradient highlight — like a model generating tokens.

Links use a vivid magenta-pink accent that pops against the dark surfaces. Motion is smooth and flowing, arriving gracefully like AI is thinking.

Purple-blue gradients became the universal signifier for AI in 2023-2024. Every startup, every pitch deck, every landing page converged on the same palette — deep purple voids, luminous blue-magenta accents, and floating glass cards. This theme captures that cultural moment in CSS.
