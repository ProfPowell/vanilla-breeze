Score Card — Sparkline composition

The sparkline slot accepts any sized element. For a true 40-pixel sparkline, an inline <svg> is the lightest, most reliable option — themed via currentColor so it inherits the trend tone. For taller trend visualisations (80px+) compose with <chart-wc>, which gives you axes, tooltips, and a screen-reader-accessible table fallback.
