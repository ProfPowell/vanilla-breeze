Solarized Theme

Ethan Schoonover's precisely engineered 16-color palette. Both light and dark modes feel equally intentional, with mathematically balanced contrast ratios.

Typography & Content

Solarized uses system sans-serif typography — the palette is the star, not the typeface. Every color has been selected for specific contrast relationships against both light and dark backgrounds.

Solarized reduces brightness contrast but retains contrasting hues for syntax highlighting readability. The result is a palette that reduces eye strain during extended use.

Links use the signature Solarized Blue which is one of eight accent colors, each chosen for perceptual uniformity across both modes.

Accordion: Design Philosophy

Solarized was designed with both light and dark modes in mind from the start. The base colors are symmetric — swapping the background and text colors between modes while accent colors remain identical.

Accordion: The 16-Color System

Eight monotone base colors provide backgrounds and text at carefully calibrated lightness levels. Eight accent colors — yellow, orange, red, magenta, violet, blue, cyan, and green — provide semantic meaning while maintaining consistent perceptual contrast.
