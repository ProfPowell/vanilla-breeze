Nord Theme

Arctic blue-gray palette inspired by the polar night and northern lights. Professional calm with muted tones and frost-blue accents.

Typography & Content

The Nord theme uses system sans-serif typography for a clean, professional feel. Body text is set in a comfortable reading weight with generous line height.

The color palette draws from the arctic landscape — polar-night darks, snowstorm lights, frost blues, and aurora-inspired accents.

Links use the frost-blue primary color with a subtle underline that strengthens on hover. The overall effect is calm and deliberate.

Accordion: Nord Design Philosophy

Nord focuses on clean, clear visuals inspired by arctic and polar aesthetics. The palette is carefully curated to be easy on the eyes during long working sessions while maintaining excellent readability.

Accordion: Color Origins

The four Polar Night colors provide the dark backgrounds, six Snow Storm colors handle light surfaces, four Frost colors define the blue accent range, and four Aurora colors add semantic meaning for success, warning, and error states.
