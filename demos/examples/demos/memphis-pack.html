MEMPHIS

Memphis Group (1981–88) aesthetic: bold flat colour, geometric patterns on every surface, hard drop shadows, zigzag borders, anti-functionalist ornament.

Colour Palette

Bold saturated primaries and secondaries applied in flat fields. No gradients, no subtlety.

Pattern System

The heart of Memphis. Every surface can carry a pattern — diagonal stripes, polka dots, squiggles, checkerboard, crosshatch, zigzag, confetti. All pure CSS.
