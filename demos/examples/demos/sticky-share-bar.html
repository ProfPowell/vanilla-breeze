The sticky share bar pattern

A vertical share rail that sticks to the side as readers scroll through an article. It's pure position: sticky composition — no JS, no scroll listeners, no IntersectionObserver.

The rail is a sibling of the article body inside a grid container. align-self: start keeps it pinned at the top of the scroll range; inset-block-start sets the offset from the viewport edge.

Scroll down — the rail follows. The trick is that position: sticky respects its containing block, so the rail stops sticking when the article ends.

At narrow widths the rail flips to a horizontal bar above the article, still sticky from the top.
