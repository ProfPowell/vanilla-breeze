Horizontal scroll section

Scroll down — the cards below translate sideways as the section moves through the viewport.

  1. Step 1

    Sticky vertical scroll, horizontal-translate child. A tall outer stage controls how long the horizontal pass takes.

  2. Step 2

    Each card is 70vw wide; the row contains four. The translate end value is calculated to leave the last card centered.

  3. Step 3

    animation-timeline: view() on a named timeline lets the row drive its own animation from the stage's scroll position.

  4. Step 4

    Reduced-motion users get a stacked, wrapped row with no horizontal motion.

The vertical scroll never feels hijacked because the browser still scrolls normally — the row just animates in response.
