Sticky hero with scale-out

The hero stays pinned while the user scrolls past it, scaling down and fading as it exits.

Content below the stage

The wrapping .stage is twice the viewport tall, so the sticky hero stays pinned across the second screenful while animation-timeline: view() drives the shrink and fade based on exit progress.

Browsers without scroll-driven animation support see a normal sticky hero that releases when the stage ends. No broken layout.
