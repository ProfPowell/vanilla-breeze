Brand Guidelines

Logo Usage

  • Use the full logo at minimum 32px height on light backgrounds with adequate clear space.
  • Don't stretch, rotate, or place the logo on busy photographic backgrounds.
  • Use the monochrome logo variant for single-color applications.
  • Don't change the logo colors or add effects like drop shadows or gradients.

Writing Style

  • Short, direct sentences with precise measurements: "±0.1 ppm accuracy."
  • Verbose academic language: "The atmospheric concentration reading has indicated an elevated level."
