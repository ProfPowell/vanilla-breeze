Vanilla Breeze / Theme System

MEMPHIS

Inspired by the Memphis Group (Milan, 1981–88) led by Ettore Sottsass. Bold flat colour, geometric surface patterns on every face, hard drop shadows, zigzag borders, and anti-functionalist ornament. Post-modernism at its most joyful.

01

Colour Palette

03

Typography

Memphis catalogues and labels used Futura Black and Futura Heavy — bold geometric letterforms, large and unapologetic. Boogaloo approximates this spirit. Space Mono for readouts.

Display — Boogaloo (≈ Futura Black)

MEMPHIS GROUP

Display / Multi-colour treatment

BOLD. FLAT. FUN.

Body — Outfit 500

Memphis rejected the austere good taste of Modernism. Sottsass declared: "Design is a way of discussing life." Every surface became an opportunity for pattern, colour, and wit.

Mono — Space Mono (data / code)

Framework

Vanilla Breeze

HTML-first, zero build step. Platform-native CSS and JS working together with the joyful chaos of a Memphis pattern — rich, purposeful, and without unnecessary complexity.

Hypermedia

html-star

Server-driven UI with zero JavaScript overhead. Like the Memphis Group working with industrial laminates — bold results from simple materials.

Reactivity

Montane

Alpine.js alternative on pure web standards. Lightweight client-side reactivity — functional at its core, decorative in its expression.
