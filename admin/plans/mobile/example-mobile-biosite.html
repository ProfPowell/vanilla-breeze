Clinical Pipeline

Two novel therapies. Measurable lives changed.

Our pipeline targets two of the most underserved conditions in neuropsychiatry, leveraging proprietary insights into serotonin modulation and neuroimmunology.

Phase 3 · Pivotal

NVR-401

ElyvaMood™

Major Depressive Disorder (MDD)

A first-in-class selective serotonin allosteric modulator (SSAM) with a differentiated tolerability profile. RADIANT-3 trial fully enrolled (n=842). Primary readout expected Q3 2026.

Target

NVR-205

SeroGuard™

Generalized Anxiety Disorder (GAD)

A neuroimmune resilience modulator targeting the gut-brain serotonin axis via a novel immunostimulatory mechanism. Induces durable anxiolytic response in preclinical and Phase 1 data.

About Nuvaris

Science-first. Patient-led. Always.

Founded in 2019 by neuroscientists from MIT and UCSF, Nuvaris emerged from a simple observation: the neurochemical basis of emotional wellness had been underinvested for a generation. We are changing that.

Headquartered in Cambridge, MA with research operations in South San Francisco, our 380-person team combines deep neuroscience expertise with disciplined clinical development to bring transformative therapies to patients who need them most.

We hold ourselves to the highest standards of scientific integrity, always.
