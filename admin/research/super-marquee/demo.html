Defensive defaults

Try the following — each is a "thing nobody else does well":

  • Switch to another tab and back. Marquees were paused while hidden (check console).
  • Scroll past a marquee. It stops animating until it returns to the viewport.
  • Tab through the page. pause-on-hover also pauses on :focus-within.
  • Enable reduced motion in your OS. All marquees pause unless explicitly reduced-motion="ignore".
  • Inspect the cloned items. They have aria-hidden, inert, and no ids.
