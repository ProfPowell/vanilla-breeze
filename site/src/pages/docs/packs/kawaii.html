Kawaii Pack

Pastel pink/mint/lavender palette, pill shapes, bouncy motion, Cherry Bomb One display font, and sparkle particle effects.

Theme

Token overrides scoped to [data-theme~="kawaii"]:

  • Pastel pink primary, mint secondary, lavender accent
  • Full pill border radii (100px)
  • Cherry Bomb One display font
  • Bouncy easing curves
  • Soft shadow tokens

Effects

Opt-in effects via data-effect attribute:

  • data-effect="starburst" — animated pastel starburst gradient background
  • data-effect="sparkle" — CSS-only floating sparkle stars
  • data-effect="bounce" — spring-loaded bounce animation on hover
  • data-effect="wiggle" — playful rotation wobble (continuous or data-trigger="hover")
  • data-effect="particles" — JS-driven floating emoji particle effect
